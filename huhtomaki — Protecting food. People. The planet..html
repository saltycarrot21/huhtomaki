Who we are · 01

A Finnish fibre company,
quietly everywhere.

We have been working with paper, pulp and plant since 1923. Today, huhtomaki packaging is opened, sipped from, peeled, lifted and lined up on shelves more than three billion times a year — across thirty-six countries, on six continents, by people who mostly don't think about packaging at all. That suits us. The work is to keep food fresher, hands cleaner and forests intact, while staying out of the way.

Signature material · 03

Pulp.
Pressed.
Possible.

Moulded fibre is what happens when paper meets water, vacuum and heat. It is breathable, shock-absorbent, recyclable, compostable, and surprisingly modern. We make it in four grades — from rough industrial buffers to surface-smooth food containers — at sites from Hämeenlinna to Hanoi.
